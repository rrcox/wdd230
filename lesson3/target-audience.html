Target Audience

All businesses in the Wayside, Ohio region which includes owners, executives, senior employees, and consultants. The chamber is a place to connect and help the community maintain a robust business environment that allows the region and its people to prosper.

Personas

New Startup Business Owner

Paula is a president and founder of new startup firm. She is 35 years old and very busy. Paula's company has less than 50 employees and really needs to hit the ground running. She often accesses websites on her phone while she is on the go and likes sites to be clean and easy to navigate especially finding contact information and schedules of upcoming networking opportunities, guest speakers, and other events. When she is at home in the evening, she uses a laptop and often takes this time to catch up on what is going on in the community.

Established Company CEO

Frank is the CEO of a well-established, large company in the area that employs over a thousand people and is a major employer in the region. He is 57 years old. Although he is technically literate, he does not like using the phone much except to make calls and text. He prefers his large screen desktop computer is his office for most of his online work. He has been asked several times to speak to the chamber and has taken the lead on many community projects. He is always on the lookup for talent and uses his connections at the chamber to find out who the up and comers are.

Scenarios

  • How can I introduce my new company to the business community?
  • What networking events are scheduled?
  • How can I make announcements about our new products?
  • What resources are available for finding potential customers?
  • What resources are available for finding potential employees?
